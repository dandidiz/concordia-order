Roleplay & Gameplay

This section is currently a WIP! I want to feel out people’s expectations for a shared server hosting an RP setting, and weigh the varied opinions with my own and what I feel capable of moderating at this time. My current thoughts are of hosting a rather lax shared space with prompts for art, literature and roleplay, with the additional tools to host mini, 1-3 chapter events as time, energy, and interest permits. The goal is that members would also be able to pitch these small campaigns to moderation and also run them. I also hope to have a ‘living’ timeline and lore document that includes world building players create or submit into the canon. Said submissions may require moderation approval to keep everything aligned with each other.

Further gamification of the space is not currently set in stone, but not something I am unfamiliar with! Workshopping an OOC currency as a reward for activity is an example of something we might introduce rather quickly if that best suits the space.

An additional pipe dream is the inclusion of ARG elements and puzzles, and a ‘collectathon’ game element, though likely on no set schedule.

This is not a set-end group with an overarching plot in mind and an outline planned down to all the fine details. It is a community hub for a world setting that’s being shared, with the intent that people feel welcome adding characters to the roster and RPing or HCing within the scope of the narrative as they, and moderation, have the energy and interest to do so. If you are looking for the former, Concordia Order will not be the right fit for you.

As things get more ironed out, this section will be rewritten to best explain the server’s purpose and scope.
