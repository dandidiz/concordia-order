Premise

The world of Concordia Order is an alternate reality of our universe in which magic exists and has always existed. It is set in a post-apocalypse of said reality, in which the general population has, for many generations now, lost connection to a previous universal ability to shift between a human form and a true self: a mythical creature form referred to as a ‘mythos’ form.

Despite the label of ‘post-apocalypse,’ the world is green and blooming and takes large amounts of inspiration from the solarpunk genre. The game is primarily set in the modern day, but the aesthetic of the world mixes current facets with that of the early 1900s.

Despite the bright nature of the world, a deadly magical corruption plagues its peoples. The RP setting largely follows the only defense against said plague: the members of Concordia Order, an organization of knights that have existed for centuries and whose traditions persist, for better or for worse, into the modern day.

Supposedly, knights alone have the arcane knowledge to cleanse the growing sickness, and they alone have access to mythos forms. The order keeps access to both abilities under lock and key, declaring them both a personal and worldly strain; widespread misuse of it could plunge the world into another apocalypse and sever even their tie to magic completely, leaving nothing left to stop the creeping threat of the ongoing threat.
