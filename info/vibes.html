Vibes

Just some silly quick doodles, and a pinterest board, of the sort of aesthetic and intent we see for this world setting! Please note we are linking the board to share inspirations of the world, not as an invitation to copy the art found within, or under the expectation the images would fit 1:1 in the world of Concordia Order. 

The world is often bright and overgrown. Well-known communities are clean, full of eco-friendly power sources, and has ease of access to community-friendly features like public transport.

Local children marvel excitedly at the novelty of seeing a knight in mythos form. 

A pair of knights find a private moment to chill and catch up on a meal.

Bibliothecaris have been called to a wealthy citizen’s home in order to contain a strange seashell. Once a decorative paperweight, it began to spout an endless stream of seawater and is currently flooding the home. A marine mythos was a must for this assignment.

A citizen feeds some of her local wildlife.

A figure interacts with a shrine from before the apocalypse event. What is the figure to them? A folk hero? An ancestor? A revered deity?
